Understanding Crisis Interactions

Crisis events create dynamic gameplay by modifying the rules of engagement. Each crisis affects factions differently, creating opportunities for strategic adaptation.

Faction-Specific Crises

Cosmic Alignment

Solaris Nexus Aeonic Dominion

Boosts units from these factions with +2 Attack and Spellshield.

Shadow Convergence

Umbral Eclipse Neuralis Conclave

Grants Stealth to units from these factions for 1 turn.

Primordial Awakening

Primordial Genesis

Empowers units with +1/+1 and Regeneration.

Global Crises

Infernal Surge

Global Effect

Deals 1 damage to all units each turn, granting Energy when they die.

Dimensional Rift

Global Effect

Reduces the battlefield to 2 lanes, returning units from disabled lanes.

Celestial Eclipse

Global Effect

Reduces Ultimate ability costs by 2 Momentum.

Strategic Implications

  • Faction Synergy: Some crises specifically empower certain factions, rewarding faction-focused decks.
  • Adaptability: Successful decks can handle both faction-specific and global crises.
  • Timing: Plan your plays around crisis duration and effects.
  • Counterplay: Some cards can manipulate or prevent crisis effects.
